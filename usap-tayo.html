Frequently Asked Questions
Common questions about counseling sessions
Is everything I say kept confidential? ›
Yes. All sessions with the guidance counselor are strictly confidential. Information shared during a session will not be disclosed to anyone without your consent, except in cases where your safety or the safety of others is at risk.
Do I need an appointment to see the counselor? ›
No, you may walk in to the Guidance Office during office hours (Mon–Fri, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM). However, submitting a request through this form helps the counselor prepare for your session in advance.
What topics can I talk about with the counselor? ›
You can discuss academic concerns, personal or family problems, mental health issues, career guidance, bullying, peer relationships, or any other concern that affects your well-being and school performance.
Will my teacher or parents be told? ›
Sessions are confidential. Parents or teachers are only notified in exceptional circumstances — for example, if there is an immediate risk to your safety or the safety of others.
How long does a session usually take? ›
Sessions typically last 30 to 60 minutes depending on the concern. Follow-up sessions may be arranged if needed.
